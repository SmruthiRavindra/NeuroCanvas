üß≠ How It Works

NeuroCanvas is built on a simple, powerful loop: Analyze ‚Üí Adapt ‚Üí Co-Create.

Analyze: From the moment you start, the app listens to your voice and observes your interactions. It maps cues (e.g., low tone, fast typing) to emotional states (e.g., sadness, anxiety).

Adapt: The AI companion responds instantly. It might change the UI theme to be more calming, recommend a warm color palette, or provide a supportive message.

Co-Create: In the "Creative Hub," the AI provides suggestions that match your emotional state, allowing you to be the main artist while the AI acts as your creative muse.
